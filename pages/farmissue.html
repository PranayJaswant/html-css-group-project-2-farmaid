Farm Issue Detection

Understanding Farm Issues

Farming is the backbone of our country, but farmers face many problems.

Early detection helps save crops and increase production.

  • Crop Diseases: Fungal, bacterial, and viral infections can damage crops.
  • Pests: Insects and rodents can eat leaves, fruits, and roots.
  • Weeds: Compete with crops for nutrients and water.
  • Soil Issues: Nutrient deficiencies and poor drainage affect growth.
  • Weather Problems: Droughts, floods, and storms can harm crops.
